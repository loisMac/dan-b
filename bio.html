Artist Bio

Dan Brown is a performance artist based in Glasgow. His work investigates human behaviour, identity, and emotional storytelling through physical performance and movement. He has collaborated with national and international artists and theatre companies, crafting immersive and experimental works that explore the relationships between body, space, and audience.
